About Career Guidance

Early awareness helps students choose subject groups and plan for engineering, medicine, agriculture, arts, commerce, paramedical, and vocational paths. Use the quiz to discover interests — then check stream-specific steps below.

Career Paths (Tamil Nadu focus)

  • Engineering — TNEA counselling after +2 (seek diploma/polytechnic or 4-year BE/BTech). Consider both government and private colleges.
  • Medicine & Allied (MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, Paramedical) — NEET for MBBS/BDS; paramedical courses via state institutions and private colleges.
  • Agriculture & Veterinary — TNAU & TANUVAS pathways for BSc Agriculture, B.Tech (Agricultural Engineering), BVSc.
  • Arts & Science — BSc, BA, BCom — many strong Tamil Nadu colleges and university-affiliated campuses.
  • Polytechnic & Vocational — TNDTE diplomas, ITI, skill courses for early employment or lateral entry to engineering.
  • Commerce & Professional — CA, CS, CMA, BCom → professional paths and business/finance roles.
  • Design, Media & IT — UI/UX, Animation, Computer Science, Data Science; many private institutes and university courses.
  • Government Jobs — SSC, TNPSC, Railways; good option after +2 or degree with targeted coaching.

Courses after 10th / 12th

After 10th: ITI, Polytechnic Diploma, HSC (Academic/Vocational)
After 12th (Science): BE/BTech, MBBS/Paramedical, BSc
After 12th (Commerce): BCom, BBA, CA foundation, professional diplomas
After 12th (Arts): BA, BFA, Social Science & teaching diplomas

Scholarships & Support (examples)

  • State scholarships: BC/MBC, SC/ST scholarships — check TN government education portals.
  • Central schemes: National Means-cum-Merit (NMMS), scholarship portals.
  • Merit awards: School & state merit-based awards for high performers.
